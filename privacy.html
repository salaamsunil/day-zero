Privacy Policy

Last updated: 11 February 2026

Day Zero Solutions ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information when you use our website at day-zero.com.au.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you voluntarily provide it through our contact form, chatbot, or waitlist signup:

  • Name - so we can address you personally
  • Email address - so we can respond to your enquiry
  • Message content - the details of your enquiry
  • Product interest - which product you're interested in

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide to:

  • Respond to your enquiries and provide product information
  • Notify you about product launches you've expressed interest in (e.g., QueueZero waitlist)
  • Improve our products and services

We will never sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate our website:

  • Formsubmit.co - processes form submissions and delivers them to our email. Their privacy policy is available at formsubmit.co/privacy.pdf.
  • Google Analytics (GA4) - collects anonymous usage data (pages visited, session duration, device type) to help us understand how visitors use our site. Google's privacy policy is at policies.google.com/privacy.
  • GitHub Pages - hosts our website. GitHub's privacy statement is at docs.github.com.

4. Cookies

Our website may use cookies set by Google Analytics to collect anonymous usage statistics. These cookies do not contain personal information. You can disable cookies in your browser settings at any time.

5. Data Retention

We retain contact form submissions and waitlist signups for as long as necessary to respond to your enquiry or until you request deletion. Analytics data is retained according to Google Analytics' default retention settings.

6. Your Rights

Under Australian privacy law, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your information
  • Withdraw consent for future communications

7. Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your personal information from misuse, interference, loss, and unauthorised access. Our website is served over HTTPS and our data is hosted in Australia.

8. Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy or want to exercise your rights, contact us:

  • Email: hello@day-zero.com.au
  • Phone: +61 415 825 955

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision.
