About

The person behind Day Zero.

SR

I'm Sunil Reddy, founder of Day Zero Solutions. By day, I lead enterprise software, automation, and AI transformation programs across Australian hospitality and food service. Day Zero is the work I do in parallel: an Australian software studio building purpose-built tools for the operators I have worked alongside for the last decade.

Why Day Zero exists

I have spent the last decade and a half running enterprise transformation programs across telecoms, financial services, and most recently Australian hospitality and aged care. Meal management platforms, inventory at scale, POS rollouts, sustainability programs, HACCP, and food waste systems across senior living, contract catering, and major stadia events. From inside that work, the same pattern keeps surfacing.

Enterprise budgets buy software that fits Australia. Independent operators, the smaller facilities, restaurants, cafés, and food trucks, run on tools designed for a different cheque size and a different market. The regulatory landscape is the same. The operator pain is the same. The cheque is not.

Day Zero exists to close that gap.

I am not building this alone

Sixteen years in enterprise transformation has put me in the room with engineers, product managers, solution managers, business leaders, area, state, and national managers, general managers, culinary leaders, and executive chefs across food service and aged care. Many of those people still guide the work. Day Zero is a studio brand: the products carry their inputs as much as mine.

What is on the platform

  • NutriCare: a dietary safety platform for aged care. IDDSI texture compliance, MUST malnutrition screening, allergen and medication-food interaction warnings, FHIR R4 export, family portal, and audit trails for Aged Care Quality Standard 6 evidence. Privacy Act 1988 aligned, with Australian data residency.
  • RestroAI: an operating system for independent restaurants. Procurement, recipe costing, online ordering with Stripe, kitchen display, HACCP, predictive ordering, multi-location, and white-label. POS connectors for Square, Toast, and Lightspeed.
  • QueueZero: mobile ordering for food trucks and pop-ups.

How I work

I would rather be straight with you than oversell. The work happens with the operators who use the products, listening closely to what does not fit and shipping the rest. If you run a facility, a kitchen, or a truck and something here resonates, email is the best way to reach me.
